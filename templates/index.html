Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our template library.

What do I get with the Free tier?

Create a free account with your email and you'll have permanent access to every template we tag as "Free tier." You can also browse the full library — titles, descriptions, and previews — so you know exactly what's available before upgrading. Free accounts also receive our monthly newsletter announcing new releases.

What's the difference between Free and Consultant?

Each template in the library is tagged as either Free or Consultant. Free-tier content is available to all registered users. Consultant-tier content — including premium role matrices, ABAP scripts, SailPoint integration configs, BPDs, and training decks — requires an active Consultant subscription. Free users can see what's in the Consultant tier, but downloading those files requires an upgrade.

How does annual billing work?

The annual plan is $1,433/year (paid upfront), which saves you 40% compared to paying $199/month. That works out to roughly $119/month. Both plans include identical access — the annual option simply costs less and locks in your rate for 12 months. You can switch between monthly and annual at any time.

Can I use these templates on client engagements?

Yes. All templates — Free and Consultant — are licensed for use on your consulting projects and client engagements. You may customize and deploy them in client environments. Redistribution of the templates themselves (selling or sharing the original files) is not permitted. See our Terms of Service for full licensing details.

Who is the Enterprise tier for?

Enterprise is designed for in-house SAP security and GRC teams — people who are capable of reading and understanding these deliverables, but want an experienced consultant to validate that the documents are correct for their specific environment before they present them internally. The Consultant tier gives you the deliverables. The Enterprise tier gives you the deliverables plus 1–5 hours per week of direct time with a senior consultant who reviews your customized templates, joins your team meetings, advises on architectural decisions, and provides written deliverables from every session. It's the confidence that comes from having an expert in your corner. Contact us to discuss your team's needs.

Can I request a specific template?

Consultant subscribers can request templates via email at info@vividConsulting.INFO. We review requests monthly and prioritize templates that serve a broad audience. New templates are added every month, drawn from real-world consulting engagements.
