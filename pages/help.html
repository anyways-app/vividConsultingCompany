People Reach Out for All Kinds of Reasons

Here are some of the situations we hear about regularly.

In-House Teams
→

"Our external auditors flagged 47 SoD conflicts in our last review and we don't have the internal expertise to remediate them before the next cycle. Can someone review the findings and build us a remediation plan?"

→

"We moved to S/4HANA but kept the ECC security model. Now Fiori apps are exposing authorization gaps we never saw before. We need someone to redesign the role structure for the new UX layer."

→

"We've been asked to stand up a GRC Process Control instance in parallel with Access Control. Our team knows AC but we've never done PC. Can you scope it and tell us what we're looking at?"

→

"Leadership wants a business case for SailPoint IIQ to replace our manual access certification process. I need someone who's done this before to help me build the justification deck."

Independent Consultants
→

"I'm on a Basis/security engagement and the client just added SailPoint IIQ provisioning to the scope. I've never built SAP connectors in SailPoint — I need someone who can own that piece while I handle the rest."

→

"I have a pharma client that needs a full GRC Access Control ruleset review — probably 25-30 hours of detailed analysis. Can you run the analysis and deliver a report I can present under my firm's name?"

→

"Three engagements just overlapped and I need a senior SAP security resource for a 12-week role redesign at a utilities company. White-label, your work under my deliverables."

→

"My client is migrating from ECC to S/4HANA and I need help with the security cutover plan. I know ECC authorization inside and out, but the Fiori catalog and PFCG changes in S/4 are new territory for me."
