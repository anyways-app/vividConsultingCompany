Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through contact forms, subscription requests, and other interactions with our website and services. This includes:

  • Your name and email address
  • Company name and business information
  • Phone number (when provided)
  • Job title and industry
  • Information about your consulting needs and interests

We also automatically collect certain usage data through analytics tools, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the website. This helps us understand how visitors use our site and improve our services.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Processing subscription requests and managing your account
  • Sending you information about our services, updates, and offers (with your consent)
  • Improving our website, services, and content based on usage patterns
  • Analyzing trends and tracking usage statistics
  • Complying with legal obligations and resolving disputes

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. We use basic analytics services to track website usage and performance. These tools may set cookies to collect information about your visits, including pages viewed, referral sources, and time spent on the site.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels for marketing purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences at any time.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encrypted transmission, secure servers, and restricted access to personal data.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You assume the risk of any data transmission.

Third Parties

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services. These include:

  • Payment Processing: We use Stripe to process subscription payments. Your payment information is handled according to Stripe's privacy practices.
  • Email & Marketing: We may use email service providers to send communications
  • Analytics: We use analytics platforms to understand website usage

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us and are required to maintain the confidentiality of your data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request that we update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information provided below.
