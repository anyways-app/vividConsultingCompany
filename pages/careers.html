Why Work With Us

vividConsulting is a small, expert team focused on delivering exceptional SAP security, GRC, and identity management consulting to enterprise clients. We don't do big, bloated consulting firms — we do targeted expertise, client relationships, and real impact.

Our team is distributed across the US and works primarily remote. We collaborate with clients on multi-month engagements, deep technical work, and strategic security architecture projects. If you're a consultant who wants to work on meaningful problems with sophisticated clients, this is the place.

What We Value

  • + Ability to work independently and manage client relationships
  • + Strong communication with non-technical stakeholders
  • + Willingness to learn and stay current with tech

What We Offer

  • + Remote work with flexibility in location

  • + Ongoing training and certification support
