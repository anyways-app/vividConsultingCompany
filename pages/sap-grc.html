SAP GRC Services

End-to-end governance, risk, and compliance solutions tailored to your enterprise.

GRC Access Control

Implementation and upgrade of SAP GRC Access Control (AC). Authorization modeling, role design, Segregation of Duties (SoD) conflict resolution, and emergency access management.

Process Control & Risk

SAP GRC Process Control (PC) and Risk Management systems. Control design, risk assessment frameworks, process mapping, and continuous monitoring setup.

Ruleset & SoD Analysis

Custom ruleset design and optimization. Segregation of Duties analysis, conflict resolution, firefighter access policies, and business role management.

Compliance Workflow Design

End-user access reviews, role owner certifications, and compliance sign-off workflows. Audit trails, attestation frameworks, and remediation tracking.

GRC Sales Consulting

Help selecting the right GRC solution. Requirements assessment, vendor evaluation, contract negotiation support, and ROI analysis for SAP GRC modules.

Emergency Access Management

Firefighter access policies and procedures. Break-glass authorization, audit logging, SoD waiver frameworks, and rapid access provisioning for critical production incidents.

How We Work

A proven methodology for successful GRC implementations from discovery to ongoing support.

1

Assess

Deep dive into your current authorization landscape, GRC maturity, compliance gaps, and system architecture. Identify pain points and define your GRC vision.

2

Design

Build your target-state GRC solution. Role architecture, SoD ruleset design, control frameworks, compliance workflows, and system configuration blueprints.

3

Implement

Execute the deployment. Configure SAP GRC modules, load data, validate rulesets, conduct testing, train end-users, and go live with support.

4

Support

Ongoing monitoring, optimization, and enhancement. SoD analysis, performance tuning, quarterly reviews, and continuous improvement cycles.
