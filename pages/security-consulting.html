Our Security Services

Comprehensive security consulting covering role architecture, authorization analysis, audit remediation, and ongoing support.

Role Architecture Redesign

Transform position-based role models into efficient task-based structures. We analyze your current authorization landscape, identify redundancy and overlap, and design scalable role hierarchies that reduce complexity while maintaining security controls.

  • Position-to-task model conversion
  • Role compression & consolidation
  • Scalability & flexibility planning

Authorization Object Analysis & Optimization

Conduct deep dives into PFCG authorization objects, SAP_* and custom objects. We map business functions to technical authorizations, identify redundant/unused objects, optimize field values, and design efficient grant strategies.

  • Object usage analysis & mapping
  • Field-level value optimization
  • Unused object identification

Segregation of Duties (SoD) Analysis & Remediation

Identify and remediate segregation of duties violations across your SAP system. We perform comprehensive SoD analysis using both standard and custom rules, provide detailed violation reports with risk assessment, and design remediation strategies.

  • SoD ruleset design & compliance
  • Violation detection & reporting
  • Risk-based remediation planning

Security Audit Preparation & Remediation

Prepare your SAP environment for external audits. We conduct pre-audit reviews, identify compliance gaps, execute remediation work, and provide documentation packages that demonstrate control effectiveness to auditors.

  • Pre-audit gap assessment
  • Control remediation execution
  • Audit documentation packages

User Access Reviews & Certifications

Execute comprehensive user access reviews and certification campaigns. We manage the process from user data extraction through certification, exception handling, and access revocation, ensuring accountability and compliance.

  • User-role reconciliation
  • Manager certification campaigns
  • Access revocation tracking

Sensitive & Critical Authorization Management

Implement controls and monitoring for sensitive transactions and critical authorizations. We design role-based frameworks for high-risk functions (RFC, batch jobs, ABAP, etc.) with enhanced approval workflows and audit trails.

  • High-risk transaction identification
  • Enhanced approval workflows
  • Comprehensive audit logging

Ongoing Security Support & Maintenance

Maintain your security posture with subscription-based support and maintenance contracts. We provide on-demand consulting, quarterly reviews, role audits, compliance updates, and proactive optimization work.

  • Quarterly security reviews
  • On-demand advisory hours
  • Subscription template access
