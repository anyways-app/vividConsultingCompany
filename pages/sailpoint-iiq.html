SailPoint IIQ Services

Comprehensive identity governance solutions for enterprise access management and compliance.

IIQ Implementation & Configuration

End-to-end SailPoint IIQ deployment including system architecture design, connector configuration, rule customization, and workflow development. From green-field implementations to major version upgrades.

Identity Lifecycle Management

Joiner, mover, leaver (JML) processes, identity creation and maintenance workflows, automatic provisioning and de-provisioning, lifecycle policy design, and event-driven provisioning across HR and SAP systems.

Access Certification Campaigns

Design and execution of annual and ad-hoc access certification campaigns. Role-based and entitlement-based certification templates, escalation policies, remediation workflows, and compliance audit reporting.

Role Mining & Entitlement Reviews

Data-driven role discovery, separation of duties analysis, role consolidation recommendations, privilege entitlement matrixing, and business role vs. technical role alignment across SAP and enterprise systems.

SailPoint-to-SAP Integration

SAP R/3 and S/4HANA connector configuration, user and role provisioning, authorization object mapping, password synchronization, HR system integration (SuccessFactors, Workday), and change data capture.

Provisioning Policy Design

Automated provisioning rule design, entitlement policy customization, conflict resolution, approval routing, delegation workflows, and exception handling for complex multi-system environments.

Compliance Reporting & Audit

SOX, HIPAA, and PCI DSS compliance reporting, audit trail configuration, attestation workflows, evidence collection, access review documentation, and regulatory compliance assessments.

Ongoing Support & Optimization

Production support, performance tuning, connector optimization, rule refinement, quarterly business reviews, capability expansion, and staying current with SailPoint platform updates.

Core Integration Strategy

SAP + SailPoint
Unified Identity

SailPoint IIQ acts as your enterprise identity hub, bridging human identity from HR systems with SAP security controls. We design connectors, map authorization objects, and ensure real-time provisioning across your SAP landscape.

SAP User Provisioning

Automated creation, modification, and deletion of SAP user accounts based on HR data and role assignments.

Authorization Object Mapping

Strategic role-to-profile mapping that aligns SailPoint roles with SAP authorization objects and transaction security.

HR-IIQ-SAP Workflow Automation

Event-driven workflows triggered by HR changes (hire, transfer, termination) that flow identity and access through to SAP.

GRC + IIQ Compliance Bridge

Integration with SAP GRC Access Control for real-time SoD violation detection and certification workflow alignment.
